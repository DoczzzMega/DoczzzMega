### About

I'm Roman — a self-taught full stack developer with over 3 years of experience. Everything I know was learned through free online resources, documentation, and building real projects.

I work across the full web development lifecycle: frontend interfaces with **React** and **Vue**, backend systems with **Laravel**, databases with **MySQL** and **Redis**, and containerized deployment via **Docker** on **Ubuntu** servers.

My understanding of how web vulnerabilities are exploited in practice informs the way I build — secure architecture is not an afterthought, it's built into the foundation.

Currently shipping projects at the intersection of **web development** and **AI**.
